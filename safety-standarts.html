Benevest Child Safety Standards

Our Commitment

Benevest is committed to protecting children and vulnerable groups. We have a zero-tolerance policy towards any form of child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE).

Prohibited Content

The following activities are strictly prohibited on Benevest:

  • Child sexual abuse material (CSAM)
  • Exploitation or endangerment of minors
  • Campaigns that promote, enable, or benefit from child exploitation

Monitoring and Enforcement

All campaigns and user activities on Benevest are monitored. Any report or evidence of CSAE will result in:

  • Immediate account suspension or termination
  • Removal of related content
  • Reporting to regional and national authorities

Reporting

Users can report any child safety concerns directly within the Benevest app. Reports are handled with urgency, and verified cases are escalated to the relevant authorities.

Compliance

Benevest complies with all relevant international and regional child protection laws. We work closely with trusted organizations to ensure a safe platform for everyone.
